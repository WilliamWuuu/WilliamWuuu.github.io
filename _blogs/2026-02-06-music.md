In 2025, I journeyed through over 300 albums—a massive soundscape that reflects more than just numbers, but hundreds of unique emotional adventures. Distilling such a vast collection, alongside timeless favorites from previous years, into a definitive "Top 10" was an agonizingly difficult task. Every omission felt like a tough call, but the albums that remain on this list are the ones that truly defined the soundtrack of my soul this year.
